Bitcoin Features

Bitcoin is a type of digital currency in which a record of transactions is maintained and new units of currency are generated by the computational solution of mathematical problems, and which operates independently of a central bank.

Fast Transaction

The Bitcoin network processes payments almost instantaneously, it just a few minutes.

Secure and Stable

The Bitcoin technology (the protocol and the cryptography) has a strong security record

Decentralised

One of Bitcoin main objective is to be independence from any governing authorities.

Non-repudiable

Once you send your Bitcoins to someone, there is no way of getting them back.

Transparent

The anonymity of Bitcoin is only relative, as every transaction is stored in the Blockchain

Anonymous

Bitcoin wallets are not necessarily linked to any personally identifying information
